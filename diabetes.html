Diabetes

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a condition in which blood sugar levels become too high.

Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst
  • Increased hunger
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision

Causes

  • Genetic factors
  • Obesity
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Insulin resistance

Risk Factors

  • Family history of diabetes
  • Being overweight
  • Unhealthy diet
  • Lack of exercise

What to Eat

  • Vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Lean protein
  • Fresh fruits in moderation

What to Avoid

  • Sugary drinks
  • Excess sweets
  • Processed foods
  • Junk food

Exercise

Walking, cycling, yoga and regular physical activity can help control blood sugar levels.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Manage stress

Complications

  • Heart disease
  • Kidney disease
  • Eye problems
  • Nerve damage

Prevention

A healthy diet, regular exercise and maintaining a healthy weight can help prevent diabetes.

When to Visit a Doctor

Consult a doctor if you experience excessive thirst, frequent urination, unexplained weight loss or persistent fatigue.

FAQ

Can diabetes be cured?
Diabetes can be managed effectively, but treatment depends on the type of diabetes.

Can exercise help?
Yes, regular exercise helps control blood sugar levels.
