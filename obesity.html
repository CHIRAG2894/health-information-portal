Obesity

What is Obesity?

Obesity is a condition in which excess body fat accumulates to a level that may negatively affect health.

Symptoms

  • Excess body weight
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty with physical activity
  • Joint pain

Causes

  • Overeating
  • Lack of physical activity
  • Genetic factors
  • Unhealthy lifestyle habits

Risk Factors

  • Family history of obesity
  • Poor diet
  • Lack of exercise
  • Insufficient sleep

What to Eat

  • Fresh fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Lean protein

What to Avoid

  • Fast food
  • Sugary drinks
  • Excess sweets
  • Highly processed foods

Exercise

Walking, cycling, swimming and regular physical activity can help with weight management.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Eat balanced meals
  • Exercise regularly
  • Get adequate sleep
  • Reduce stress

Complications

  • Diabetes
  • Heart disease
  • High blood pressure
  • Joint problems

Prevention

A healthy diet, regular exercise and maintaining a healthy lifestyle can help prevent obesity.

When to Visit a Doctor

Consult a doctor if weight gain is affecting your health or daily activities.

FAQ

Can obesity be treated?
Yes, obesity can often be managed through healthy eating, exercise and medical guidance.

Can exercise help?
Yes, regular exercise is important for weight control and overall health.
