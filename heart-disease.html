Heart Disease

What is Heart Disease?

Heart disease refers to conditions that affect the heart and blood vessels, reducing the heart's ability to function properly.

Symptoms

  • Chest pain
  • Shortness of breath
  • Fatigue
  • Irregular heartbeat
  • Dizziness

Causes

  • High blood pressure
  • High cholesterol
  • Smoking
  • Diabetes

Risk Factors

  • Family history of heart disease
  • Obesity
  • Lack of exercise
  • Unhealthy diet

What to Eat

  • Fresh fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Whole grains
  • Lean protein

What to Avoid

  • Fried foods
  • Processed foods
  • Excess salt
  • Sugary drinks

Exercise

Walking, cycling and regular physical activity help maintain heart health.

Lifestyle Changes

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Exercise regularly
  • Quit smoking
  • Manage stress

Complications

  • Heart attack
  • Heart failure
  • Stroke
  • Irregular heartbeat

Prevention

A healthy diet, regular exercise and avoiding smoking can help prevent heart disease.

When to Visit a Doctor

Consult a doctor if you experience chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness or irregular heartbeat.

FAQ

Can heart disease be prevented?
Many forms of heart disease can be prevented through healthy lifestyle choices.

Can exercise help?
Yes, regular exercise helps improve heart health and reduce risk factors.
